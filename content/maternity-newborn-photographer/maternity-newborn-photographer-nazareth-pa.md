## FAQs for Maternity & Newborn Photography in Nazareth, PA

**When should I schedule my maternity session?**  
The ideal time for maternity portraits is between 28-34 weeks, when your beautiful bump is prominent but you're still comfortable moving around. We'll schedule based on your specific due date and how you're feeling.

**How soon after birth should we do newborn photos?**  
The optimal window for newborn portraits is within the first 14 days of life, when babies sleep more soundly and naturally curl into those adorable poses. However, beautiful images can be created at any age!

**Is newborn photography safe?**  
Absolutely. I've completed specialized newborn safety training and always prioritize your baby's comfort and wellbeing. The studio is kept warm, sessions are paced around feeding and soothing, and I never force a pose.

**Do you provide maternity gowns and newborn props?**
