## FAQs for Senior Portraits in Bethlehem, PA

**When should I schedule my senior portraits?**  
Many students book in late spring or summer before senior year. Fall is popular for beautiful colors but books up quickly. If you need photos for fall yearbook submission, book by early summer.

**Can I bring props related to my activities?**  
Absolutely! Bring sports equipment, musical instruments, letter jackets, or anything that represents your interests and accomplishments. Props add personality and make your portraits unique.

**What should I wear for my senior photos?**  
Bring 2-3 outfits that represent your style—mix casual and dressy, incorporate your school colors if desired, and choose solid colors or subtle patterns that won't date your photos. Avoid large logos.

**Can my friends or siblings join for some photos?**  
Yes! We can incorporate a few shots with friends, siblings, or even pets. Just let me know in advance so I can plan appropriate time.
