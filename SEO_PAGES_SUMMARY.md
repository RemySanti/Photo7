## Content Structure

Each page includes the following sections:

1. **Front Matter**: service, city, slug, title_tag, meta_description, h1, word_count_goal
2. **H1 Heading**: Repeats the h1 from front matter
3. **Intro**: 2-3 paragraphs introducing the service and city with CTA
4. **Why Choose Us**: 3-5 bullet points focusing on benefits
5. **Popular Locations**: City-specific landmarks and photo locations
6. **What's Included**: Service-specific session details
7. **How the Process Works**: 3-step process with internal links
8. **FAQs**: 4 service and location-specific Q&A pairs
9. **Call to Action**: Compelling closing paragraph with CTA

## SEO Optimization Features

- Natural keyword placement throughout content
- Location-specific landmarks and details for each city
- Varied intros, CTAs, and "Why Choose Us" sections to avoid duplicate content
- Proper heading hierarchy (only one H1, multiple H2s)
- Word count goal of 700 words per page
- Service + city combination used 2-3 times per page naturally
- Meta descriptions under 155 characters
- Mobile-friendly markdown format
